Billion Prices project collects online and offline prices of selected products sold by selected retailers. 
It was founded in 2008 by Alberto Cavallo and Roberto Rigobon and aims to collect price information all over the world.

More information: http://www.thebillionpricesproject.com/

The original data is Stata file (.dta). In the clean folder, we have it as is in .dta and exported to .csv, too.
